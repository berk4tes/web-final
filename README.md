# 🌙 Luma — AI Mood Recommendation Platform

> *Your mood, curated.*

Luma is an AI-powered entertainment discovery platform that recommends **movies, music, books, and series** based on your mood, energy, and emotional vibe.  
Instead of searching endlessly, users explore curated “vibes” designed around feelings like nostalgic, dreamy, vibrant, calm, or intense.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🎬 Mood-based movie & series recommendations
- 🎵 Curated music playlists
- 📚 Interactive book shelf experience
- 🎨 AI-generated moodboards
- 📅 Mood tracking calendar
- 🏆 Gamified vibe system & leaderboard
- 🌌 Cinematic glassmorphism UI
- ⚡ Real-time interactive dashboard
